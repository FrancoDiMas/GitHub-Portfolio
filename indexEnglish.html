¿How did I find out about programming?

When I was 17 years old I was in an online game called Tibia, this game became my favorite for a long time, inevitably I found some players who moved automatically, structuredly and did not respond to my messages, which led me to investigate further. Over time I discovered that they were characters called "Bot's", which were programmed by people who, like me, they were in love with the game and were a developer/programmer as a profession. This is how I got to know programming, since then it always amazed me and caught my attention, by then I saw it as complex, a year later I started trying to program a "Bot" in LUA language, being extremely difficult but having success, surprising so to some of the people who programmed because of my poorly written, inexperienced but incredibly creative and effective code. For some reason I didn't think I could learn it on my own. I had a few other attempts but the more functions it was more complex without an education... as the years passed, I looked at programming as a dream that captured my attention a lot but I had not proposed it as a purpose to learn. Many years later, calmly I realized that I had an ability to program but I was not able to see it clearly at that time, with this came the fact of investigating more and seeing it as a profession acquiring my first Java course on Udemy. Then I learned Python (so far one of the ones I've loved the most) and finally decided to learn frontend; HTML, CSS, JavaScript and along with backend using Python (flask), MYSQL.
